生效日期：2026年07月13日

一、前言

欢迎使用爱己自由（Love Myself Live Free）心灵疗愈应用。我们高度重视您的个人信息与隐私安全，本政策将清晰说明我们收集、使用、存储、保护您信息的全部规则。使用本App即代表您同意本隐私政策全部条款。

二、我们收集哪些信息

仅收集您主动自愿提交的内容，不会私自调取手机通讯录、定位、相册、麦克风等权限：

  1. 注册账号信息：您填写的登录邮箱，用于账号登录、找回密码、接收服务通知；
  2. 情绪记录内容：心情日记、感恩日记、魔法盒子内填写的文字；
  3. 创作内容：心灵画板绘制的图像；
  4. AI交互内容：您与AI灵伴对话产生的文字记录；
  5. 设备基础标识：匿名设备ID，用于统计使用频次、优化产品功能，无法定位真实个人身份。

三、信息使用目的

  1. 账号服务：完成登录、账号验证、找回账号、推送产品更新通知；
  2. 核心功能运行：展示情绪报表、AI心灵陪伴、冥想疗愈、情绪收纳等功能；
  3. 产品优化：匿名化统计使用数据，优化界面、修复功能故障；
  4. 安全防护：识别异常登录、防止账号被盗用。

四、信息存储与保护

  1. 数据存储：您的个人内容将加密存储，仅您本人可查看；
  2. 留存期限：账号存续期间保存全部内容；您注销账号后，我们将在7个工作日内永久清除您所有个人数据，无法恢复；
  3. 安全防护：采用行业标准加密技术传输、存储数据，杜绝非法访问、泄露、篡改用户信息。

五、信息共享规则

我们 绝不会出售、出租、交易 您的任何个人信息。仅以下两种特殊场景有限共享：

  1. 获得您单独书面同意后，才会向第三方传递您的内容；
  2. 司法机关依法出具正式文书要求调取数据时，我们将配合提供对应信息。

六、您拥有的隐私权利

  1. 访问权：随时在App内查看自己全部日记、创作、对话记录；
  2. 删除权：可单条删除单篇内容，也可通过注销账号一键清空所有个人数据；
  3. 修改权：随时修改日记、画板创作内容；
  4. 撤回同意：您可随时停止使用App，注销账号终止全部数据收集。

七、未成年人保护

本App面向18周岁以上成年用户使用。若未满18周岁，请在监护人知情并同意的前提下使用，请勿单独提交个人信息。

八、政策更新

我们会不定期更新本隐私政策，更新后将在App内推送通知，持续使用App即视为您接受更新后的条款。

Love Myself Live Free Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Love Myself Live Free, a mental healing application. We fully respect and protect your personal privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, store and safeguard your data. By accessing and using our app, you agree to all terms of this privacy policy.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect data you actively submit, and we will never access your phone contacts, location, gallery or microphone without your permission:

  1. Account data: Your login email for sign-in, password recovery and service notifications;
  2. Emotional records: Texts written in mood journal, gratitude journal and Magic Box;
  3. Creative content: Drawings created in Soul Canvas;
  4. AI chat data: Conversation records between you and AI Soul Companion;
  5. Anonymous device ID: Used only for anonymous usage statistics and product optimization, which cannot identify your real identity.

3. How We Use Your Data

  1. Account service: Support login, identity verification, account recovery and app update notifications;
  2. Core feature operation: Generate mood reports, provide AI mental companion, meditation and emotional release functions;
  3. Product optimization: Analyze anonymous usage data to fix bugs and improve user experience;
  4. Security protection: Detect abnormal login to avoid account theft.

4. Data Storage & Security

  1. Encrypted storage: All your personal content is stored with industry-standard encryption, only visible to yourself;
  2. Data retention: Your records are kept while your account exists. After you delete your account, all personal data will be permanently erased within 7 working days and cannot be recovered;
  3. Security measures: We use encryption technology to prevent unauthorized access, leakage and modification of user data.

5. Data Sharing Statement

We will never sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third parties. Data will only be shared in the following limited situations:

  1. We obtain your separate explicit consent before sharing your content with third parties;
  2. We comply with formal legal requests issued by judicial authorities.

6. Your Privacy Rights

  1. Access right: You can view all your journals, drawings and chat records inside the app at any time;
  2. Deletion right: Delete single entries separately, or clear all personal data by deleting your account;
  3. Editing right: Modify your journal texts and canvas works anytime;
  4. Consent withdrawal: You may stop using the app and delete your account to terminate all data collection.

7. Minor Protection

This application is intended for users over 18 years old. If you are under 18, please use this app with full awareness and consent of your legal guardian, and do not submit personal information alone.

8. Policy Modification

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Notice will be posted inside the app after revision. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or personal data processing, please contact the developer via email: modefang_2023@qq.com
